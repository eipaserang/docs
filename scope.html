Scope
Electric-Instrument Oleo/Non-Edible handles works related with 3 things :

1. Electrical

Electrical is all things related with power, such as : high/medium/low voltage panels, MCC, motor, lighting, VFD, UPS, air conditioning, etc

2. Instrumentation

Instrumentation is all things related with instruments and control system, such as : flowmeters, level transmitters, temperature transmitters, pressure transmitters, control valves, on-off valves, other instuments, DCS, and PLC.

3. Special Equipment

Special equipment is equipment with special control system where there are some combinations between E&I and mechanical, such as : robot, compressor, separator, flare system, packing machine, chiller, hp boiler, etc
